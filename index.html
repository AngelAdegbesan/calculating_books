How Much is the New York Times Best Seller's List?

By Angel Adegbesan

For many authors, the prestigious New York Times’ best seller’s list is where you want your book to be, simply because it represents the best written books. Being on the list usually means the books fly off the shelves. For avid book readers, the list, published weekly, is a representation of new (or sometimes old), insightful and thought-provoking books. Readers search through this list world wide to get the next best read.

The list varies from fiction to nonfiction, from children’s books to thrillers and from hardcovers to e-books. This article compares the list for the week of March 21, 2021.
